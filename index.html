Making a difference

Our products are all handmade in the biggest slum in Asia- The infamous Dharavi Slum in Mumbai, India. Although structurally illegal, Dharavi is home to over more than a million people. It also an incredible centre of creativity- from leathers, jewellery, embroidery, clothing, food and much more, Dharavi is an economic hub with small scale industry thriving in the area.

However, exploitation takes place with some people getting paid next to nothing for their work.

We are working with women in the slum to help them build a reputable business with fair pay and help and advice on how to make best use of their finances for their independence and their children's future.
